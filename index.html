UX & UI Designer

I am an entrepreneur and UX professional. Having launched and run a successful start-up, I understand the importance of crafting a good user experience and why telling a good story is essential to success. I follow a rigorous yet adaptable UX process and have learnt from some of the best in the business.

User Experience

For any project I start by building an understanding of the core premise and strategy. I then use digital research software, workshops and clearly presented analysis to build outcomes with long-term value.

Developer

My focus with code is the design, behaviour and feel of a product. HTML, CSS and Javascript are my tools when the budget is tight, but the insight I have into the challenges that developers face has proved invaluable, especially when it comes to complex project delivery by large, multifaceted teams.

Fun

On top of my excitement about how we use and experience products, I trained as an artist, so I find it very natural to develop the design of a brand. I enjoy building long-form interactive stories and have done some just for fun. Like this. I live in Berlin and love to paint, run and read in my spare time.

More

I am fluent with Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign, and have experience with Omnigraffle and plenty of other wireframing tools. I’m also familiar with prototyping software such as Invision and Proto.io.

 

I’m comfortable with public speaking and with making presentations and can create workshops with confidence.

 

I love to apply my drawing and design skills, and I’m happy dealing with clients and executives alike.

 

I’m always looking for a project that tells a good story.
